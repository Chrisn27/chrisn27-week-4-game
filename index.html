Puppy Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four puppies below. By clicking on a puppy you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it

The target score and value of the puppies with change each time the game starts
